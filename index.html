Hello! My name is Leighton and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2019 when I decided to start my own buisness selling products online through shopify — although i had some success i learned designing websites was what i most enjoyed.

After deciding i wanted to go into software engineering i first looked at going back to university to study computer science. I then discovered that there is a plethora of online resources showing aspiring programmers how to code and eventually how to land your first role.
